EMT requirements

  • 16 years old or older
  • CPR certified
  • Pass a Rig Test
  • Have an EMT license

EMT Certificates

I got EMT certified at Safety-EMS and had incredible instructors! My class started on May 23, 2024 and ended on August 10, 2024. EMT class was every Thursday 6:30pm-10:30pm, and 7:30am-4pm every Saturdays!

After an EMT class is taken, you must pass the NREMT, the National Registry EMT exam, in order to practice in the state of New Jersey. You can take it at a Pearson Center!

Watch this video to learn more about Marlboro EMS! Marlboro EMS is always looking for new members.

As an EMT, respond to 911 emergency calls, transport patients to hospitals, and work with other first responders.
